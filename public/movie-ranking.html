Top Best Movies of all time

Kimi No Na Wa (Your Name) *2016*

The best animated romance and fiction history of all time, describe the history of Taki and Mitsuha.
Two lovers separated for the time but reunited for the destiny.

The Lion King *1994*

The history of Simba a Lion prince exiled by his uncle (Scar) who killed his father Mufasa (The king) and stole the kingdom,
Scar believes that he killed his nephew Simba but a few years after Simba comes back to recover what belongs to him.

Similar to the history of Hamlet but with very good a Twist.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone *2001*

The story of Harry Potter the boy who lived, is a wonderful history of magic and adventure, Harry Potter lose his family,
and after years he discovers that he is in fact a wizard, and he starts going to Hogwarts, a wizard school where
Harry have a lot of adventures and discovers the secrets about the death of his parents and the truth about his lighting scar.
